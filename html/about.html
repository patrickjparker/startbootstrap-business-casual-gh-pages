Kenna Customs About the Artist

Hi! My name is McKenna, and I believe that the most ordinary moments can become extraordinary with the right pair of shoes.

When I was younger, I always loved art. I started my first business in elementary school, selling hand-sewn designs out of my backpack, and I have been creating ever since. I currently attend Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah where I plan to graduate in Experience Design and Management. From painting shoes to performing music, experiencing the outdoors, or creating websites, my greatest passion is using my influence as a designer to make a positive impact.
